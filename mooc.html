NPTEL Swayam is an initiative by the Indian government to provide free online courses in various subjects. It is a collaboration between NPTEL (National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning) and SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active-Learning for Young Aspiring Minds). The platform offers high-quality educational content and certification to learners across India.


Coursera is an online learning platform that partners with top universities and organizations to offer a wide range of courses, specializations, and degrees. It provides learners with the opportunity to gain knowledge and skills from renowned institutions and earn certificates upon completion.


Mookit is an online learning platform that offers massive open online courses (MOOCs) in a variety of subjects. It provides learners with the opportunity to access high-quality educational content from top universities and institutions around the world.s


IITBX IITBX (IIT BombayX) is an online learning platform that offers courses from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. It provides learners with the opportunity to access high-quality educational content in various subjects, including engineering, science, and technology. IITBX aims to make quality education accessible to a wider audience through online courses and interactive learning experiences.


Open2Study is an online learning platform that offers free courses in various disciplines, from business and marketing to science and technology. It provides learners with the opportunity to access high-quality educational content, interact with instructors and fellow students, and earn certificates of completion. Open2Study aims to make education accessible to all, regardless of location or financial constraints.


Udemy is an online learning platform that offers a vast range of courses taught by expert instructors in various fields, including business, technology, and creative arts. It provides learners with the flexibility to learn at their own pace and access course materials anytime, anywhere. Udemy also offers a certificate of completion for each course to showcase acquired skills.


WizIQ is an online learning platform that offers virtual classrooms, courses, and live tutoring services. It provides educators and learners with a platform to connect and engage in interactive online learning experiences. WizIQ also offers features such as video lectures, assessments, and collaborative tools to enhance the learning experience.


EdX is an online learning platform that offers a wide range of courses, certifications, and even degree programs from top universities and institutions. It provides learners with the opportunity to gain knowledge and skills in various subjects through interactive and engaging course materials.
